## âš¡ How to Play

1. Open `index.html` in your browser.
2. Select the game mode:
   - **Player vs Player**: Two players take turns clicking on empty cells.
   - **Player vs Computer (CPU)**: Play against the AI which makes random moves.
3. The game starts with **Player X** by default.
4. **Making a Move**:
   - Click on an empty cell to place your mark (X or O).
   - The current player is highlighted in the status area.
5. **Winning Conditions**:
   - A player wins if they align **three of their marks horizontally, vertically, or diagonally**.
   - The game announces the winner and stops further moves.
6. **Draw**:
   - If all cells are filled and no one has won, the game declares a **draw**.
7. **CPU Behavior**:
   - In Player vs Computer mode, the AI plays as **O**.
   - The AI picks an empty cell randomly.
   - The game announces if the AI wins or if itâ€™s a draw.
8. **Restarting the Game**:
   - Click the **Restart Game** button at any time to reset the board.
   - Player X will always start after restarting.
